Frequently Asked Questions

Why are my prayer times incorrect?

Please ensure that you have granted location permissions so the app can calculate accurate times for your specific city. You can change this in iOS Settings > IftarTimer > Location.

Does the app need internet?

No, IftarTimer uses localized calculation methods to predict prayer times without requiring an active internet connection after the initial setup.

How do I change the calculation method?

Go to the Settings tab within the IftarTimer app and select 'Calculation Method' to choose the one most appropriate for your region (e.g., Diyanet).
